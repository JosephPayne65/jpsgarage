Our Services

From routine maintenance to complex repairs — we've got you covered.

All Services

Oil & Fluid Services

Conventional and synthetic oil changes, coolant flushes, transmission fluid, brake fluid, power steering fluid, and differential services.

Brake System

Brake pad and rotor inspection/replacement, brake line repair, ABS diagnostics, caliper service, and complete brake fluid flushes.

Engine Diagnostics

Check engine light analysis, OBD-II scanning, performance testing, and manufacturer-level diagnostic reporting.

Engine Repair

Head gasket repair, timing belt/chain replacement, valve work, cylinder service, and full or partial engine rebuilds.

Transmission

Automatic and manual transmission repair, fluid service, clutch replacement, and torque converter service.

Electrical Systems

Battery testing and replacement, alternator and starter repair, wiring diagnostics, fuse and relay service.

Tire & Wheel

New tire installation, rotation, balancing, four-wheel alignment, TPMS service, and flat repair.

Heating & Cooling

A/C recharge, compressor replacement, heater core repair, radiator service, thermostat replacement, and hose inspection.

Suspension & Steering

Shocks and struts, ball joints, tie rods, control arms, power steering pump repair, and rack and pinion service.

Exhaust System

Muffler replacement, catalytic converter service, exhaust manifold repair, and emissions system diagnosis.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled tune-ups, spark plug replacement, air and cabin filter changes, belt and hose inspection.

Safety Inspections

Comprehensive vehicle safety checks, pre-purchase inspections, and state-required emissions testing.

Not sure what your vehicle needs? Bring it in — we'll diagnose the issue and walk you through your options with honest, transparent pricing.
