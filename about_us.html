About JP's Garage

Aurora's trusted neighborhood auto repair shop — built on honesty, skill, and community.

Our Story

JP's Garage was founded on a simple idea: that every vehicle owner deserves to be treated with respect, and every repair should come with straight talk and fair pricing. No upsells, no surprises — just quality work done right.

What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into Aurora's go-to destination for reliable automotive care. We've built our reputation one satisfied customer at a time, and we're proud of every vehicle that leaves our bay in better shape than it came in.

Whether it's a quick oil change or a complex engine repair, you'll always know what we're doing, why we're doing it, and exactly what it'll cost — before we ever pick up a wrench.

What We Stand For

Honesty

We'll tell you exactly what your vehicle needs — and what it doesn't. You'll never walk out with a repair you didn't need.

Quality

We use quality parts and proven techniques on every job. We stand behind our work because your safety depends on it.

Transparency

You'll get a clear estimate before we begin. No hidden fees, no last-minute additions — just the price we quoted.

Community

We're your neighbors. Aurora is our home, and we take pride in keeping the cars on its roads safe and road-ready.
